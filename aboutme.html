On LLM Tools:

Large Language Models are incredibly powerful at streamlining busywork, finding missing semicolons, and replicating previously existing code. However, these tools often fail to handle complex tasks and can be deceptive in their "correctness." While the power offered by LLMs as tools is vast, they don't have the capacity to critically engage with complex systems, especially as they are being developed. LLMs can solve small problems lightning quick, but only human beings can solve big ones.
